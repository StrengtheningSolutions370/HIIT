About Us

Bester Strength and Conditioning (BSC) was established in 2020 by Neil Bester, who is a qualified sports scientist after completing his undergraduate studies at the University of Johannesburg, as well as his postgraduate studies at Wits University.

Bester Strength and Conditioning opened with the intention of providing functional fitness classes to both ordinary people and professional athletes. The gym offers individual training sessions, as well as group classes. The individual training sessions are client specific and focus on enhancing certain aspects of an individuals' fitness, whilst group classes consist of multiple clients who complete a given set of exercises in order to achieve their fitness goals.
