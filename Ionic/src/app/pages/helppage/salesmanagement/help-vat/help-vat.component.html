Create VAT
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter VAT entry information

3. Click on the "Create VAT" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search VAT
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Delete VAT
Steps:

1. Slide the VAT entry you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete VAT" button
