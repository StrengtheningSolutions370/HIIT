Create Refund Reason
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter refund reason information

3. Click on the "Create Refund Reason" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Refund Reason
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Refund Reason
Steps:

1. Slide the refund reason you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the refund reason information

4. Click on the "Update Refund Reason" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Refund Reason
Steps:

1. Slide the refund reason you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Refund Reason" button
