Create Write-off Reason
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter write-off reason information

3. Click on the "Create Write-off Reason" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Write-off Reason
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Write-off Reason
Steps:

1. Slide the write-off reason you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the write-off reason information

4. Click on the "Update Write-off Reason" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Write-off Reason
Steps:

1. Slide the write-off reason you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Write-off Reason" button
