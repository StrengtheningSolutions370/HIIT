Create Sale Category
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter sale category information

3. Click on the "Create Sale Category" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Sale Category
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Sale Category
Steps:

1. Slide the sale category you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the sale category information

4. Click on the "Update Sale Category" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Sale Category
Steps:

1. Slide the sale category you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Sale Category" button

4. If there are sale items currently associated to the sale category, it will not be able to delete. The associated sale items need to be deleted first.
