Create Lesson
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter lesson information

3. Click on the "Create Lesson" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Lesson
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Lesson
Steps:

1. Slide the lesson you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the lesson information

4. Click on the "Update Lesson" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Lesson
Steps:

1. Slide the lesson you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Lesson" button
