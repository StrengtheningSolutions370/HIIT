Create Supplier
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter supplier information

3. Click on the "Create Supplier" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Supplier
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Supplier
Steps:

1. Click on the “Update” button

2. Update the supplier information

3. Click on the "Update Supplier" button

4. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Supplier
Steps:

1. Click on the “Delete” button

2. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Supplier" button
