Record Measurements
Steps:

1. Click on the "Measurements" menu item

2. Click on the "Add Measurement" button

3. Enter exercise category information

4. Click on the “Add” button

5. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Generate Performance Report
Steps:

1. Click on the "Measurements" menu item

2. Select the date range

3. Click on the “Download” button
