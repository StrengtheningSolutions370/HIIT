Create Title
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter title information

3. Click on the "Create Title" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, then click on the "Confirm" button


Search Title
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Title
Steps:

1. Slide the title you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update Title” button

3. Update the title information

4. Click on the "Update Title” button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Title
Steps:

1. Slide the title you wish to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Title” button

4. If there are users currently associated to the title, it will not be able to delete. The associated users need to be deleted first.
