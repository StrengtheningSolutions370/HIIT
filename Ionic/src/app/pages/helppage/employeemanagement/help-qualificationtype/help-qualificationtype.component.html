Create Qualification Type
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter qualification type information

3. Click on the "Create Qualification Type" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Qualification Type
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Qualification Type
Steps:

1. Slide the qualification type you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the qualification type information

4. Click on the "Update Qualification Type" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Qualification Type
Steps:

1. Slide the qualification type you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Qualification Type" button
