Create Qualification
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter qualification information

3. Click on the "Create Qualification" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Qualification
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Qualification
Steps:

1. Slide the qualification you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the qualification information

4. Click on the "Update Qualification" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Qualification
Steps:

1. Slide the qualification you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Qualification" button
