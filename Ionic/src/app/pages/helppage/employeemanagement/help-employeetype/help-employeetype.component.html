Create Employee Type
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter employee type information

3. Click on the "Create Employee Type" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Employee Type
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Employee Type
Steps:

1. Slide the employee type you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the employee type information

4. Click on the "Update Employee Type" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Employee Type
Steps:

1. Slide the employee type you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Employee Type" button

4. If there are employees currently associated to the employee type, it will not be able to delete. The associated employees need to be deleted first.
