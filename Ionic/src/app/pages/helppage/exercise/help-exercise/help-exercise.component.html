Create Exercise
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter exercise information

3. Click on the "Create Exercise" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Exercise
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Exercise
Steps:

1. Slide the exercise you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the exercise information

4. Click on the "Update Exercise" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Exercise
Steps:

1. Slide the exercise you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Exercise" button
