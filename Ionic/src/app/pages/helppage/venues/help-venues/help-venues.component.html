Create Venue
Steps:

1. Click on the “+” button

2. Enter venue information

3. Click on the "Create Venue" button

4. Read and confirm the entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Search Venue
Steps:

1. Click on the search input field

2. Enter a search criteria


Update Venue
Steps:

1. Slide the venue you want to update to your left

2. Click on the “Update” button

3. Update the venue information

4. Click on the "Update Venue" button

5. Read and confirm the newly entered information, and click on the "Confirm" button


Delete Venue
Steps:

1. Slide the venue you want to delete to your left

2. Click on the “Delete” button

3. Confirm deletion by clicking on the "Delete Venue" button
